🎉 **New badge app: !Fri3d Friends** 🎉

Find your people at camp! A little app for the Fri3d badge that does three things:

🟢 **Nametag** — your name big, your group(s) as colourful pills
📡 **Friend finder** — quietly buzzes and lights an LED when someone from one of your groups comes into Bluetooth range (each nearby friend gets their own softly-breathing LED in their group's colour)
🤝 **Contact swap** — you and a friend both press **Y** and your badges exchange contact info (Discord, phone, website… whatever you add), saved with the date you met

Set everything up from your phone through the badge's little WiFi web portal — no painful badge-keyboard typing.

Works on **both the 2024 and 2026 badges**. Install it from the **AppStore** on your badge. Open source (MIT): https://github.com/steemandavid/fri3d-friends

Made with ❤️ by David Steeman / Makerspace Baasrode — feedback & PRs welcome!
